About

I'm Zak! I was born near Lake of the Ozarks, Missouri, raised by the Rocky Mountains of Missoula, Montana, and educated in the small town of Richland, Missouri (A mere pitstop between Springfield, MO and Lake of the Ozarks). I moved to Kansas City, MO at the age of 17 promptly after my high school graduation to join a church plant internship as well as write and play music in a local band. During this time I grew in love with the city and decided to make it my home. Interests of mine include: reading philosophical/theological content, playing games with friends and family, coffee, writing and playing music, chess, basketball, building Discord servers, and solving problems with code. I have a wife and two boys and they are very much my whole world.
